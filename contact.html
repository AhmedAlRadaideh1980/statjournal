Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process typically take?

The typical review process takes 4-6 weeks from submission to initial decision. This includes initial editorial screening, peer review, and editorial decision. Complex manuscripts or those requiring major revisions may take longer.

Do you charge article processing fees?

No, AI-Stat Journal is a platinum open access journal with no article processing charges (APCs) or submission fees. All articles are freely available to readers without any costs to authors.

How do I declare AI assistance in my manuscript?

Authors must declare all AI assistance in the dedicated section of the submission form. This includes specifying the AI model, version, provider, and describing the nature of assistance. Please refer to our AI Co-Authorship Policy for detailed guidelines.

Can I submit a manuscript that is currently under review elsewhere?

No, we do not accept manuscripts that are currently under consideration at another journal. Simultaneous submission is considered a violation of publication ethics. You may submit to AI-Stat Journal only after receiving a formal rejection from another journal or withdrawing your submission.

How can I become a reviewer for the journal?

We welcome applications from qualified researchers interested in joining our reviewer panel. Please contact us with your CV and areas of expertise, or indicate your interest when creating an author account in our submission system.
