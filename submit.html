Ethics and Compliance

Submission Requirements

Before submitting, please ensure your manuscript meets the following requirements:

  • Manuscript follows the journal's formatting guidelines
  • All authors have approved the submission
  • Manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere
  • AI assistance is properly declared (if applicable)
  • All references are properly cited
  • Data availability statement is included (if applicable)
  • Conflict of interest statement is included
  • Funding sources are acknowledged

For detailed submission guidelines, please refer to our Author Guidelines.
