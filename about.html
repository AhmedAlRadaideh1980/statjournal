Mission and Scope

The AI-Stat Journal is an open-access, peer-reviewed scientific journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research at the intersection of statistics, data science, and AI-assisted analytics. Our mission is to advance the field by publishing innovative research that demonstrates the practical application of statistical methods enhanced by artificial intelligence.

We pioneer the inclusion of AI co-authorship, recognizing transparent and ethical AI contributions in scientific research while maintaining rigorous academic standards.

Journal Focus Areas

  • Statistical Methodology: Novel statistical models, inference techniques, and computational statistics
  • Machine Learning & AI: AI algorithms, deep learning, and their statistical foundations
  • Data Science Applications: Real-world applications in business, healthcare, social sciences, and engineering
  • Intelligent Analytics: AI-enhanced data analysis, visualization, and interpretation
  • Ethical AI & Statistics: Fairness, transparency, and ethical considerations in AI and statistical practice

Types of Manuscripts Accepted

  • Research Articles: Original research presenting significant contributions
  • Review Articles: Comprehensive surveys of current research areas
  • Methodology Papers: New statistical methods or substantial improvements to existing ones
  • Case Studies: Detailed applications of statistical and AI methods to real problems
  • Short Communications: Brief reports of preliminary findings or technical notes
  • Software Papers: Descriptions of new statistical software or AI tools

Peer-Review Process

All submissions undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process. Our editorial board consists of internationally recognized experts who ensure that published articles meet the highest standards of academic excellence.

The typical review process includes:

  • Initial editorial screening for scope and formatting
  • Assignment to at least two independent reviewers
  • Double-blind review process (typically 4-6 weeks)
  • Editorial decision based on reviewer recommendations
  • Revision period for conditionally accepted papers
  • Final acceptance and publication

Open Access Policy

AI-Stat Journal is a platinum open access journal, meaning:

  • All articles are immediately freely available to read, download, and share
  • No article processing charges (APCs) or submission fees
  • Authors retain copyright of their work
  • All content published under Creative Commons Attribution License (CC BY)

This model ensures maximum visibility and impact for published research while removing financial barriers for both authors and readers.

Indexing and Impact
