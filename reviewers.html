Reviewer Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when reviewing manuscripts for AI-Stat Journal:

Review Process

  • Reviews should be completed within 3 weeks of assignment
  • Provide constructive, specific, and evidence-based feedback
  • Evaluate both strengths and weaknesses of the manuscript
  • Maintain confidentiality - do not share manuscript details
  • Declare any conflicts of interest to the editor

Evaluation Criteria

Category Evaluation Points
Originality Novelty of research, contribution to field, innovative methodology
Methodology Appropriateness of methods, statistical rigor, reproducibility
Results Clarity of presentation, appropriate analysis, validity of conclusions
AI Use Proper disclosure, ethical use, appropriate application
Writing Quality Clarity, organization, grammar, appropriate references

AI-Specific Considerations

When reviewing manuscripts with AI involvement:

  • Verify that AI use is properly disclosed according to our policy
  • Assess whether AI contributions are appropriately described
  • Evaluate if human oversight is evident in the work
  • Check that AI-generated content has been properly verified
  • Consider whether the AI use adds value to the research

Review Submission

Please use our online review form to submit your evaluation. The form includes:

  • Overall recommendation (Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject)
  • Rating scales for key evaluation criteria
  • Comments to authors (constructive feedback for improvement)
  • Confidential comments to editors (e.g., ethical concerns, reviewer conflicts)
  • Option to upload annotated manuscript (optional)

Recommendation Guidelines

  • Accept: Manuscript is excellent and requires no revisions
  • Minor Revision: Manuscript is strong with minor issues that need addressing
  • Major Revision: Manuscript has potential but requires substantial improvements
  • Reject: Manuscript has fundamental flaws or does not fit journal scope

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my review be?

Reviews typically range from 500-1500 words, focusing on the most important aspects of the manuscript. Quality is more important than length.

What if I cannot complete a review on time?

Please contact the editorial office as soon as possible if you need an extension or must decline a review assignment.

Can I suggest additional reviewers?

Yes, you can suggest other qualified reviewers in your confidential comments to the editor.

Is reviewer training available?

Yes, we offer optional online training sessions for new reviewers. Contact the editorial office for details.
