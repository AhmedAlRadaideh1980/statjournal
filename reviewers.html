Reviewer Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when reviewing manuscripts for AI-Stat Journal:

Review Focus Areas

  • Originality: Does the paper present novel concepts, approaches, or results?
  • Significance: Are the findings important to the field of statistics and AI?
  • Methodology: Are the methods sound, appropriate, and well-described?
  • AI Transparency: Is AI assistance properly declared and documented?
  • Clarity: Is the paper well-organized and clearly written?
  • References: Are citations appropriate and complete?

AI-Assisted Manuscripts

When reviewing manuscripts with AI assistance:

  • Verify that AI use is properly declared in the submission
  • Assess whether the level of AI assistance is appropriate and ethical
  • Evaluate the human intellectual contribution beyond AI-generated content
  • Check for coherence and consistency that might indicate insufficient human oversight

Review Structure

Please structure your review as follows:

  1. Summary: Brief overview of the paper's main contributions
  2. Major Comments: Substantive issues that affect the paper's validity or significance
  3. Minor Comments: Specific suggestions for improvement (typos, clarifications, etc.)
  4. Recommendation: Clear recommendation (Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject)

Review Criteria

Please use the following criteria when evaluating manuscripts:

Criterion Excellent Good Fair Poor
Originality & Innovation Highly novel approach or concept Some novel elements Minor innovation No significant innovation
Technical Quality Methodologically sound and rigorous Generally sound with minor issues Some methodological concerns Major methodological flaws
Clarity & Organization Exceptionally clear and well-structured Generally clear with minor issues Requires significant improvement Unclear and poorly organized
Significance to Field Major contribution to the field Moderate contribution Minor contribution No significant contribution
AI Use (if applicable) Appropriate, transparent, and ethical Generally appropriate with minor issues Some concerns about AI use Inappropriate or unethical AI use
